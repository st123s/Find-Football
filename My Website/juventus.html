Juventus FC

General Facts

  • Founded: 1897
  • Stadium: Allianz Stadium
  • Colors: Black and White
  • Manager: Massimiliano Allegri
  • Championships: 36 Serie A titles, 14 Coppa Italia titles

Historic Achievements

Juventus FC is one of Italy's most successful and celebrated football clubs. With a record number of Serie A championships, Juve has consistently dominated Italian football. Their success isn't just confined to Italy; they've also made significant strides on the European stage.
