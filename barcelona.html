FC Barcelona

General Facts

  • Founded: 1899
  • Stadium: Camp Nou
  • Colours: Blue and Garnet
  • Manager: Xavi Hernandez
  • Championships: 26 La Liga, 31 Copa del Rey

Legendary Players

Barcelona has been home to legends like Lionel Messi, Xavi, Andrés Iniesta, and Johan Cruyff.

The MSN Trio

The MSN trio—Messi, Suárez, and Neymar—dominated world football from 2014 to 2017.

European Success

Barcelona has won 5 UEFA Champions League titles, solidifying their place among Europe's elite.

Historic Rivalry

The rivalry with Real Madrid, known as "El Clásico", is one of the fiercest in world football.
