Real Madrid CF

General Facts

  • Founded: 1902
  • Stadium: Santiago Bernabéu Stadium
  • Colors: White
  • Manager: Carlo Ancelotti
  • Championships: 35 La Liga titles, 14 UEFA Champions League titles

Iconic Players

Real Madrid has produced some of the greatest players in football history, including Alfredo Di Stéfano, Raúl, and Cristiano Ronaldo.

The Galácticos Era

The Galácticos era of Real Madrid saw world-class players like Zinedine Zidane, Ronaldo Nazário, and David Beckham represent the club.

European Dominance

Real Madrid has dominated European football with 14 UEFA Champions League titles, more than any other club in history.
